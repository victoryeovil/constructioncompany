Background Information

Iconic Concrete Masters (PVT) LTD is a wholly Zimbabwean Owned company INCOOPERATED IN 2018. Though fairly new on the market the combined expertise of its highly qualiﬁed technical and support staff makes it a service provider of choice. At Iconic Concrete Masters we specialize in the manufacturing and distribution of durable quality concrete products. Our product range includes culvert pipes, manhole rings, concrete poles, kerbstones, lintels, valve boxes amongst many others.


Vision
To be your ultimate choice in Zimbabwe and beyond for all your concrete products manufacturing requirements Our vision gives us a sense of direction and destination. It captures our aspiration of being the best in everything we do. It is the bases of what we all stand for as a company.


Mission
To provide durable long lasting concrete products to our clients at an economically viable price mechanism that ensures more value for investment
